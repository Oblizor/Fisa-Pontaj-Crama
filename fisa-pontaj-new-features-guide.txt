### 3. üí∞ PAYMENT TRACKING SYSTEM

**Purpose:** Track all money paid to employees (advances, partial payments, final payments)

**Record a Payment:**
1. Go to "Reports & Payments" tab
2. Scroll to "Record Payment / Advance" section
3. Fill in:
   - Employee Name (select from dropdown)
   - Date (when payment was made)
   - Amount (in RON)
   - Payment Type:
     - **Advance Payment**: Money paid before work is done
     - **Final Payment**: Complete settlement
     - **Partial Payment**: Part of what's owed
   - Notes (optional): e.g., "Cash payment, week 1-2"
4. Click "üí∞ Record Payment"

**Payment History:**
- All payments are listed below the form
- Shows employee name, date, amount, type, and notes
- Can delete payment records if entered incorrectly

**Integration with Reports:**
- Payment totals automatically appear in employee reports
- Balance Due = Total Earned - Total Paid
- See exactly what you still owe each employee
